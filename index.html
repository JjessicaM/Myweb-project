ABOUT ME

My name is Jessica Marlene Alcaraz Hernandez, but you can call me Marlene. I from Guadalajara, Jalisco, México and I have a degree in Communication Sciences.


My life is pretty much like every other kid during the 90’s; you know go to school, make homework, watch Dragon Ball, Sesame Street, Power Rangers, Animaniacs, and El Chavo del Ocho a Mexican TV show. Those were such a good days.

You can work a lot to make it happened, if you want to make a 30-second-commercial you spend a least nine hours, so that the part that I love the most.


Right now I’m taking a course in Web & App Development in Vancouver, Canada. So far away from home and I can say It’s hard to be that far but at the same time it’s exciting to thing where you’re going to be after finish the experience. After all for me life means experiences.
